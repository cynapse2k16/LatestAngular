SYNAPSE -The place where neurons communicate with each other. A junction between two nerve cells, consisting of a minute gap across which impulses pass by diffusion of a neurotransmitter. It is that essential space without which we would be quite Brain-Dead. Well, it is with good reason that we have replaced the ' S ' by ' C '. We proudly present 'CYNAPSE', where the C is a tribute to that vital revolution, now a part & parcel of all our lives, a creation of Dennis Ritchie - yes the C language. Its inception dates back to the year 2003 and ever since we have been working hard for it to reach out to a greater extent. The participation and popularity of Cynapse has been escalating ever since.
